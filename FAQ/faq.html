Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if I damage the car?

In the event of accidents resulting in damage to the vehicle during the rental period, the user shall be required to pay the non-operation charge (NOC) as partial compensation for the loss of earnings incurred for repairs, regardless of the degree of damage or the length of time required for repairs. The non-operation charge (NOC) is different from the deductible (paid by the user) in the insurance and compensation system, which is applied in the event of accidents. Please note that this sum is to be paid even if you are subscribed to the Safety Package.

What all documents are required for verification?

To rent an Avis self-drive car, you should have a valid Aadhaar card, a driving license and an original Passport/Voters ID card. Please note: Aadhaar card details like name, mobile number etc. should match the details that are provided to Avis at time of making a booking. In case of a mismatch, Avis will cancel the booking and a penalty of Rs 1000 will be levied to the guest in such case. Also, it is required to make sure that all the details mentioned in the Driving License and Aadhaar card are readable and clear.

What if I return the car late?

Guest needs to inform RentCaro in advance for rental extension. An extension or grace period of up to 1 hours is permissible if informed with no extra charges. If the extension exceeds 1 hours,charges will be applicable for every hour including the 1-hour grace period and will be added to the total bill.To avoid paying any extension fee we request our guest to extend the booking through the Avis India website or mobile app. This is applicable on both self-drive and chauffeur-drive

What is your cancellation policy?

As per RentCaro cancellation policy, if the booking is cancelled:

48 hours prior to pick up time No cancellation charges
Within 24 to 48 hours of pick up time 3% of the rental amount will be deducted.
Within 12 to 24 hours of pick up time 25% of the rental amount will be deducted
Within 0 to 12 hours of pick up time 50% of the rental amount will be deducted
At or after pick up time No refund
